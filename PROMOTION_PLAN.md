## 🎯 Key Messages

### Elevator Pitch
> "markrel uses Markov chains to learn document relevance from your data. It's 50x faster than neural re-rankers and actually interpretable—like a school of mackerel swimming to the most relevant documents."

### Differentiators
1. **🐟 Unique Approach**: Only library using Markov chains for relevance
2. **⚡ Speed**: 50K+ samples/sec (vs 100s for neural models)
3. **🔍 Interpretable**: See exactly why each document was selected
4. **📊 Benchmarked**: Extensive testing on WikiQA with BGE-M3

### Taglines
- "School your documents with Markov chains"
- "Fast, interpretable document relevance"
- "Your documents, swimming to relevance"
